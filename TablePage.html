The majority of this novel is set in a realm called Idavoll, a magical portal version of Midgard (Earth). Humans (called Elms in this novel) and non-magical entities remain outside of this portal. To live and exist in Idavoll, each magical creature (whether they be witch, wizard, skinwalker, stoneborne, dragon, fae, etc.) must possess a brooch. Brooches are magical gemstones, in short, that store and enhance the magical power of the user. The brooches themselves then act as Idavoll's form of iPhone (even with Apple Pay) - however described more elegantly in the novel. The stones are created by the race of Stoneborne and delivered to administration (basically magical government) where, when a being reaches their species' age of maturity will go to receive their brooch. This table will list the name of the stones, the colours of the stones, their various healing properties, and the elements to which they belong.
