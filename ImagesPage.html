Story Characters / Image Page

Mauja Le Guin

Mauja appears as an Inuit to Elms and a pink-haired demigod to the beings in Idavoll. Her mother is the Celtic Goddess of Winter and her father is a human Medicine Man. Mauja attends Magical Harvard Medical School. She has the rare ability to travel through time portals.

Gerda

Gerda is a sentient purple carpetbag mimic with infinite holding. Her mouth zippers open. Gerda is the sidekick of Mauja who acts as her doctors bag. Gerda has dog-like behaviour and is a clever protective guard. Gerda has an inappropriate eating problem.

Lady Siv

Siv is the BFF of Mauja. She is a Nordic Goddexx who has sentient golden hair named Hairy. She is the Elm liaison at the Ice Palace. Siv has attended magical Harvard 256 times. She is one of the older Goddexx as she survived Ragnarök.

Hairy

Hairy is sentient long hair made of gold that is attached to Siv. He is a kleptomaniac, and his curtain of hair has a large hold. Hairy cannot speak but can communicate telepathically with Lady Siv. He sprung Mauja from the prison bubble when she first arrived in Idavoll.

Tituba

Tituba is an Egyptian Goddexx witch. Tituba is a former enslaved witch who lived during the Salem Witch Trials. Her magic level and experience fighting Elms leads to her becoming the General of the Witch forces. She is considered the most powerful warrior in Idavoll.

The Yard Bard

The Yard Bard is a Banshee that protects the Harvard Yard at Magical Harvard. She herds students and wayward peacocks and gives tours of The Yard. She uses her screaming magic to sing students to sleep at nighttime.

Magnus

Magnus is a ginger mergecat. A mergecat is an interdimensional Cheshire cat that can travel through time and has the ability to transport others through time. Ze saves Mauja and Siv by transporting them back to their own time when they become trapped in the past.

The Snow Queen

The Snow Queen is the ruler of the North Kingdom. She was created by the former queens of the North Kingdom to be a neutral party and embody the best aspects of each queen. She is made of magical snow. After a successful quest, Mauja marries the Snow Queen.

Tanwen

Tanwen is a Welsh ten-year-old that moved to California because her mum is employed at SpaceX. She took a souvenir rock from the Welsh caves with her which happened to be a dragon egg. She holds extraordinary power.

Sriracha

Sriracha is Tanwen's emotional support lizard(?). He is loyal to Tanwen without a fault and will protect her at all cost, even if that means sprouting wings and shooting off a fireball or two.
